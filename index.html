About Me

I am Fabien. I came here 13 years ago from France after being kicked out of engineering school near Paris. I decided to start again with a clean slate. I decided to study in the bay area and, I wanted to do it on the cheap. Sadly, I did not speak english. I wrote a college application in french and used google to translate it to engligh. I then submitted it to a few public universities. I was rejected nearly everywhere. Luckily, I got accepted at Berkeley, nearly 15 miles from my house.

After graduation, I decided to stay in the US. Living in Berkeley was fun but I wanted more. I tried to live in San Francisco but could not afford it. I found a job in tech and started a real estate business in Vallejo. I also decided to live in Hayward for a year to save up. It ended up being two... I grew more bored and eventually managed to move downtown SF where my life took a new turn. I made friends all around the city. I hung out in all neighborhoods and met people from all walks of life. I also party a lot. My profile pic is a selfie of myself in a hot tub :).
